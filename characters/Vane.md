# Vane
Station Master, Ostra-Vael  

Vane is the practical counterpart to Kaelen's metaphysical role. Where Kaelen keeps the station conceptually coherent, Vane keeps it operationally functional—managing dwindling supplies, maintaining morale among three hundred souls, and making the hard calls about resource allocation. She understands that Kaelen's liturgies cost him something, but not precisely what.
