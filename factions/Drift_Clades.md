# Drift Clades

The Drift Clades are not a unified civilization but a loose category of peoples sharing a common condition: they were born in places that forget easily, and they built their cultures around that fact. Their traditions of **inheritance memory** and **clade-traditional thinning** — deliberate, ritualized release of personal history — mark them as alien to the preservation-obsessed mainstream of interstellar society.

They are brilliant precisely because impermanence forces creativity. Freed from the weight of accumulated memory, Drift Clade engineers iterate rapidly, fail without shame, and produce innovations that stable civilizations, encumbered by institutional memory, cannot conceive. Their miniaturized memory drives are ubiquitous across the known galaxy, even among factions that publicly disparage them.
