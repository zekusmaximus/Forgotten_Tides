# Remembrancers of Solace

Where the Archivist Orders guard memory and the Obsidian Synod destroys it, the Remembrancers occupy a harder middle ground: they decide, case by case, what must be let go. Their Memory Hospice facilities are among the most emotionally demanding postings in the known galaxy — places where archivists sit with the dying and help them release what cannot be carried forward.

Their neutrality is hard-won and carefully maintained. Every faction uses them as intermediaries precisely because the Remembrancers are known to refuse weaponization of what they know. In a universe where information is power and memory is currency, their refusal to exploit either makes them uniquely trusted.
