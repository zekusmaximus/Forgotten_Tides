# Obsidian Synod

The Synod does not think of itself as a criminal organization. Its members believe they are performing an act of cosmic compassion — accelerating the universe toward the peace of universal forgetting. They are patient, methodical, and genuinely devoted.

The stations they have collapsed are not, in their theology, disasters. They are completions. The predatory eddies that form in the aftermath — spiraling voids that consume nearby memory structures — are, to the Synod, sacred phenomena.

The Archivist Orders regard them as the single greatest existential threat to the Memory Web. The Canticle Fleet hunts them when they can find them. The Black Horizon makes them almost impossible to root out entirely.
