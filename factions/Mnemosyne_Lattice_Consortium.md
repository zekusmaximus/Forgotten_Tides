# Mnemosyne Lattice Consortium

The Consortium holds the most technically sophisticated memory infrastructure in the known galaxy, but their ambition outpaces their stability. They present a face of total competence to the political world while privately managing an escalating crisis: several lattice nodes in their core territory are experiencing conceptual drift they cannot explain and dare not disclose.

Their public agenda — centralize memory governance, regulate drives, eliminate unlicensed corridor work — would, if successful, make them the effective government of interstellar civilization. Every other faction regards them with a mixture of dependency and dread.
