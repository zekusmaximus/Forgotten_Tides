# Narthex of Low Mercy

The Narthex of Low Mercy is built around the Synod principle that a place should
remember only enough to keep serving release. Its corridors are plain, its
records sparse, and its strongest rooms are those designed to let memory go.

In the Black Horizon, that lightness is both doctrine and engineering.
