# Black Horizon

The Black Horizon is where the universe is actively forgetting itself. Dark energy exceeds memory-mass across vast stretches of the galactic perimeter, and the result is a region where time moves at inconsistent rates, where memories bleed through whatever containment structures pilots bring with them, and where the black-hole superclusters seem to pull not just matter but the record of matter into their interiors.

The Obsidian Synod has built its most significant installations here, precisely because the Black Horizon is nearly impossible to police. The Canticle Fleet will not send ships in without full anchor reserves and careful rotation. The Archivist Orders maintain monitoring stations on the near edge but do not venture deeper.

What the Synod has found there — what they believe the black-hole superclusters are teaching them — remains unknown to the rest of the galaxy.
