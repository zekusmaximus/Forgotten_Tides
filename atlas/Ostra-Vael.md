# Ostra-Vael

Ostra-Vael is a station whose purpose has outlived its memory. Built during the Lattice Expansion for silicate mining, it now sits empty of industry but still inhabited by those who cannot or will not leave. Its seven decks suffer progressive conceptual decay: floors soften, hatches forget their function, and corridors lose their geometry. A single Continuity Cleric performs daily liturgies to hold the structure together, making Ostra-Vael a case study in the cost of preserving something that the universe is trying to forget.
