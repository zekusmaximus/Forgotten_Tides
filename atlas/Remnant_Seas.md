# Remnant Seas

The Remnant Seas are what remains when a civilization forgets a star. The collapse is not physical — the stellar mass persists — but the memory-structure that once gave the star coherent presence in the Memory Web dissolves, and what fills that space is something stranger: scar tissue in the fabric of remembered space, shards of reliquary material that once held the star's final moments, and creatures adapted to living in the permanent absence of memory gravity.

Researchers who work in the Remnant Seas seek the oldest memories in the universe — pre-sentient structures that predate any civilization's capacity to record them. What they find, when they find anything, is fragmentary and often incomprehensible. But occasionally a shard surfaces that rewrites everything anyone thought they knew about how memory began.
