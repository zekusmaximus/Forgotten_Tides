# Mnemosyne Lattice Core

The Lattice Core is the gravitational center of the Memory Web — the densest concentration of memory-mass in the known galaxy. Corridors here are so stable they feel almost physical; pilots joke that you could navigate blind. Stations resist drift for centuries without maintenance. The architecture remembers everything.

It is, paradoxically, the most dangerous place in the galaxy to tell the truth. The Consortium controls every information channel in the Core, and its concealed crisis — the slow drift in several lattice nodes that its engineers cannot explain — is protected by institutional machinery sophisticated enough to erase uncomfortable facts before they become inconvenient ones.
