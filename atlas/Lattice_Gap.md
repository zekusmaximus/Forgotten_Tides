# Lattice Gap

The Lattice Gap is where the Memory Web is tearing. Stations here drift out of alignment — not spatially, but conceptually, as the architecture forgets its own purpose and corridors lose their harmonic signatures. The eddies that form in the Gap are different from those elsewhere: they move with apparent intent, following the scent of concentrated memory like predators.

The Heliodrome sits at the epicenter of the Gap's worst fracture zone. The Canticle Fleet treats the entire region as an active crisis requiring continuous triage. Every mission into the Gap is a calculation of what can be saved, what must be burned, and what is already beyond recall.
